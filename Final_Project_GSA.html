Designing Effective GSAs and Safe Spaces

Although our I.S 289 GSA is far from perfect, it works towards creating a safe space for all students, as reflected by some of the work.

Possible GSA work

  1. Vocabulary work

  2. Guest speakers

  3. School signage

  4. Cross grade presentations

  5. Design school safety survey

  6. Plan and run faculty PD (Professional Development)

  7. Develop GSA pop-up library

  8. Map safe/unsafe spaces

  9. Author visit - Alex Gino George

  10. Celebrate/discuss current events

  11. GSA social media/blog

Role of Teachers

  1. Teachers act as facilitators and allow students' experiences and ensure student voices are heard.

  2. They make sure that any privilege they may have as adults or if they are white and/or cis-het do not overshadow student experiences.

  3. Teachers are also people students can reach out to.
